[Catalan number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catalan_number) satisfies **recurrence relation**:

$$
C_0 = 1
\quad
\text{and}
\quad
C_{n+1} = \sum_{i=0}^{n} C_i C_{n-i}
\tag{reccurrence}
$$

And its general term formula is:

$$
C_n = \frac{1}{n+1} {2n \choose n}
$$
